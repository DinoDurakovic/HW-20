USDA in Action

The UX and UI redesign for USDA.GOV and navigation prototype

USDA provide’s leadership on food, agriculture, natural resources, rural development, nutrition, and related issues based on public policy, the best available science, and effective management. They have a vision to provide economic opportunity through innovation, helping rural America to thrive

Role

UX, UI Designer

10 user interviews/Usability Tests conducted

Given three weeks to research, design and test. Working within a limited time frame can have its challenges

Problem Statement

We believe that since USDA aims to be the leader in providing the economy opportunity, with innovation, and the best available science. we are here to help USDA conform a new navigation for their website and mobile design, to fit their users needs of being able to easily access the information they need.

New Design

Navigation

Through our user testing and user research, my team and I found that 80% couldn’t find pertinent information, regarding important topics, such as food recalls and resources. with our new redesigned navigation, our testing reported that almost all users could navigate to where they needed to go within the website. this in turn should lead to more users interacting with the site, and using USDA for all their farming and agricultural needs.
